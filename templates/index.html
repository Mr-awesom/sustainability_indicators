What's inside?

Four ready-made analyses powered by static/data/gt_full.csv and the companion CO₂ datasets.

  • NOx concentration performance, exceedance rates against a configurable limit, and monthly percentiles.
  • Load-normalised proxy emission indices (NOx/TEY and CO/TEY) trended monthly and segmented by load quartile.
  • Carbon ledger summarising tonnes emitted, carbon intensity and ETS exposure using synthetic EUA pricing.
  • Regulatory readiness scorecard that tracks the status of key EU and US compliance deliverables.
